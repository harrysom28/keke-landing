About us

What is it about US?

Keke is your fast, affordable way to ride. We connect passengers with skilled drivers and offer flexible earning opportunities for drivers. Choose from a range of categories and prices — no surge tricks, just fair rides.

Our Mission

To empower communities by providing a dependable and safe ride-hailing service that enhances mobility and fosters economic opportunities for tricycle, car, and motorcycle drivers.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the way people travel by offering a seamless, tech-driven transportation solution that caters to the unique needs of African cities.

Why Choose KEKE?

  • Convenience: Book a tricycle, car, or motorcycle ride with just a few taps on your smartphone, anytime, anywhere.
  • Reliability: Count on us for timely pickups and efficient routes, ensuring you reach your destination without hassle.
  • Safety: Your safety is our top priority. We have integrated emergency contact features and real-time tracking to ensure a secure ride.
  • Affordability: Enjoy cost-effective rides, making transportation accessible to all.

What We Offer?

  • Tricycles: Our primary focus, providing flexible and accessible transportation.
  • Cars: For those who prefer a more traditional ride-hailing experience.
  • Motorcycles: Perfect for quick and agile navigation through busy city streets.
